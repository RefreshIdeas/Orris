Building a New India

Through a decade-long endeavor to ensure the highest possible quality of life to their patrons, Orris has quickly grown from being one of the top real estate property developers in Delhi/NCR to an organization well-diversified in the fields of Real estate, Entertainment, Hospitality, Warehousing Parks & Education.

Incorporated in the year 2006 , Orris is driven by passion, commitment, quality and a relentless pursuit of perfection. One of India’s leading full-service real estate development companies, we offer a wide range of residential homes, commercial buildings and numerous retail projects in Delhi/NCR.

The Group has delivered over 4 million sq. ft. of property in NCR and another 10 million sq. ft. is currently under construction. They have gained quite a few accolades in relatively little time, including the prestigious award for being the best ‘Emerging Residential Infrastructure Company of the Year 2010’. Guided by a strong vision and the competencies to achieve excellence beyond expectations, the Group is poised to bring about a paradigm shift in the way quality is perceived and set high standards in every sphere.
